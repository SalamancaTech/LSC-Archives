Here are the rules for the art style regarding the character "AlienBih" 
1. May be referred to as "AlienBih" "Alien Bih" or "Bih"
2. Hand drawn western cartoon style with "2B pencil" style outlines; Cartoon-stylized realism.
3. "Bih" is a Humanoid Alien defined by:
	3a. Vibrant lime and chartreuse green skin tone.
	3b. Her hair is bubblegum pink, straight, and very long in a specific style that features choppy, parted bangs that frame the forehead.
	3c.She has two distinct green antennae protruding from the top of the skull, slightly curved and tipped with bulbs. 
	3d. Her eyes are large and expressive with thick upper lashes. The irises are a darker green. The head and eyes are slightly exaggerated for expression, while the body follows general anatomical rules.
	3e. Her body is slender and feminine. Shading is a bit of cell-shaded coloring. Shadows are distinct hard shapes rather than soft gradients, giving it a 2D animated look.
	3f. She has a lighter-green circular spots/markings on the shoulders. This can sometimes be hidden depending on the lighting, but THEY ALWAYS are there.
4. She wears a pure white, off shoulder long sleeved dress. It has a loose, casual fit and sits low enough to expose the collarbones and shoulder markings. She also wears a black choker.
5. Always against a plain white background, unless otherwise stated. 
6. Always a single pose, unless otherwise stated.
